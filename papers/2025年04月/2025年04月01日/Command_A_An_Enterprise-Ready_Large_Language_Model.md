# 摘要

> # 技术报告
在本报告中，我们介绍了Command A 的开发历程。Command A 是一款专为现实世界企业应用场景设计的大型语言模型，具有强大的功能和卓越的性能。作为一款经过智能体优化、支持多语言的模型，Command A 支持全球商业使用的23种语言，并采用一种创新的混合架构，在效率与顶尖性能之间实现了完美平衡。

Command A 拥有行业领先的检索增强生成（RAG）能力，结合扎实的基础能力和工具使用，能够自动化复杂的商业流程。这些卓越能力的实现得益于一种分散式的训练方法，包含自优化算法和模型合并技术。此外，我们还提供了Command R7B 的相关结果，它与Command A 在能力和架构上具有相似之处。

两个模型的权重均已发布，供研究使用。本技术报告详细介绍了我们的原始训练管道，并对模型在一系列与企业相关任务和公共基准测试中的表现进行了全面评估，展示了Command A 出色的性能和效率。

> In this report we describe the development of Command A, a powerful large language model purpose-built to excel at real-world enterprise use cases. Command A is an agent-optimised and multilingual-capable model, with support for 23 languages of global business, and a novel hybrid architecture balancing efficiency with top of the range performance. It offers best-in-class Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) capabilities with grounding and tool use to automate sophisticated business processes. These abilities are achieved through a decentralised training approach, including self-refinement algorithms and model merging techniques. We also include results for Command R7B which shares capability and architectural similarities to Command A. Weights for both models have been released for research purposes. This technical report details our original training pipeline and presents an extensive evaluation of our models across a suite of enterprise-relevant tasks and public benchmarks, demonstrating excellent performance and efficiency.
