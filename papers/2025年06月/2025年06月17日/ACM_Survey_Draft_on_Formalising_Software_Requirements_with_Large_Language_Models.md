# 摘要

> 本草稿是一份工作文档，包含对九十四篇论文的综述，并新增了软件需求可追溯性（第4节）、形式化方法及其工具（第5节）、编程统一理论（UTP）及机构理论（第6节）等部分。请参考[7,8]的摘要。本草稿与我们近期预期的同名文献的主要区别在于：[7]是一份提交给爱尔兰ADAPT年度会议的两页论文，于2025年3月18日提交，经过轻量级盲审后被接受为海报展示，会议于2025年5月15日举行。[8]是一份九页的论文，附带九页参考文献和摘要表格，于2025年4月24日提交至人工智能验证研讨会（SAIV 2025）。该论文经过严格的审稿流程。arXiv.org上发布的版本[8]是经过改进的提交版本，采纳了审稿人提出的改进建议。

> This draft is a working document, having a summary of nighty-four (94) papers with additional sections on Traceability of Software Requirements (Section 4), Formal Methods and Its Tools (Section 5), Unifying Theories of Programming (UTP) and Theory of Institutions (Section 6). Please refer to abstract of [7,8]. Key difference of this draft from our recently anticipated ones with similar titles, i.e. AACS 2025 [7] and SAIV 2025 [8] is:
  [7] is a two page submission to ADAPT Annual Conference, Ireland. Submitted on 18th of March, 2025, it went through the light-weight blind review and accepted for poster presentation. Conference was held on 15th of May, 2025.
  [8] is a nine page paper with additional nine pages of references and summary tables, submitted to Symposium on AI Verification (SAIV 2025) on 24th of April, 2025. It went through rigorous review process. The uploaded version on arXiv.org [8] is the improved one of the submission, after addressing the specific suggestions to improve the paper.
