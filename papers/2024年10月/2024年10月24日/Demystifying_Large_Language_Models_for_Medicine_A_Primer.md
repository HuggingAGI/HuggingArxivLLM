> Demystifying Large Language Models for Medicine: A Primer

# 摘要

> # 摘要
大型语言模型（LLMs）是一类具有变革性的人工智能工具，能够通过生成类人响应并适应新任务，彻底改变医疗保健的多个领域。其应用范围广泛，涵盖临床文档记录、患者与临床试验匹配及医学问题解答等任务。本文提出了一套实用指南，帮助医疗专业人员更高效地利用LLMs，并附有最佳实践。该方法包括任务制定、模型选择、提示工程、微调和部署等关键步骤。我们首先探讨了如何识别与LLMs核心能力匹配的医疗任务，并根据任务需求、数据特性、性能要求和模型接口选择合适的模型。接着，我们介绍了提示工程和微调等策略，使标准LLMs适应特定医疗任务。此外，还讨论了部署中的法规合规性、伦理指南及公平性与偏见的持续监控。通过提供结构化指导，本文旨在帮助医疗专业人员安全、可靠且高效地将LLMs应用于临床实践。

> Large language models (LLMs) represent a transformative class of AI tools capable of revolutionizing various aspects of healthcare by generating human-like responses across diverse contexts and adapting to novel tasks following human instructions. Their potential application spans a broad range of medical tasks, such as clinical documentation, matching patients to clinical trials, and answering medical questions. In this primer paper, we propose an actionable guideline to help healthcare professionals more efficiently utilize LLMs in their work, along with a set of best practices. This approach consists of several main phases, including formulating the task, choosing LLMs, prompt engineering, fine-tuning, and deployment. We start with the discussion of critical considerations in identifying healthcare tasks that align with the core capabilities of LLMs and selecting models based on the selected task and data, performance requirements, and model interface. We then review the strategies, such as prompt engineering and fine-tuning, to adapt standard LLMs to specialized medical tasks. Deployment considerations, including regulatory compliance, ethical guidelines, and continuous monitoring for fairness and bias, are also discussed. By providing a structured step-by-step methodology, this tutorial aims to equip healthcare professionals with the tools necessary to effectively integrate LLMs into clinical practice, ensuring that these powerful technologies are applied in a safe, reliable, and impactful manner.
