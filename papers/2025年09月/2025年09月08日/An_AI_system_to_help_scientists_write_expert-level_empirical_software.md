# 助力科学家编写专家级实证软件的AI系统

发布时间：2025年09月08日

`LLM应用` `医疗健康`

> An AI system to help scientists write expert-level empirical software

# 摘要

> 科学发现的进程常因支持计算实验的软件需人工缓慢开发而陷入瓶颈。为解决此问题，我们研发了一套人工智能系统，能够生成专家级科学软件，核心目标是最大化质量指标。该系统借助大型语言模型（LLM）与树搜索（TS），不仅能系统优化质量指标，还能智能游走于海量可能解空间。通过探索并融合外部复杂研究成果，该系统能实现专家级表现。树搜索的有效性已在众多基准测试中得到验证：在生物信息学领域，它成功发现40种单细胞数据分析新方法，在公开排行榜上性能超越人类开发的顶尖方法；在流行病学领域，生成14个预测模型，在COVID-19住院人数预测上，性能超越美国疾控中心（CDC）的集成模型及其他所有单个模型。此外，该方法还产出了地理空间分析、斑马鱼神经活动预测、时间序列预测及积分数值求解等领域的最前沿软件。通过为各类任务设计并落地创新解决方案，该系统为加速科学发展迈出了关键一步。

> The cycle of scientific discovery is frequently bottlenecked by the slow, manual creation of software to support computational experiments. To address this, we present an AI system that creates expert-level scientific software whose goal is to maximize a quality metric. The system uses a Large Language Model (LLM) and Tree Search (TS) to systematically improve the quality metric and intelligently navigate the large space of possible solutions. The system achieves expert-level results when it explores and integrates complex research ideas from external sources. The effectiveness of tree search is demonstrated across a wide range of benchmarks. In bioinformatics, it discovered 40 novel methods for single-cell data analysis that outperformed the top human-developed methods on a public leaderboard. In epidemiology, it generated 14 models that outperformed the CDC ensemble and all other individual models for forecasting COVID-19 hospitalizations. Our method also produced state-of-the-art software for geospatial analysis, neural activity prediction in zebrafish, time series forecasting and numerical solution of integrals. By devising and implementing novel solutions to diverse tasks, the system represents a significant step towards accelerating scientific progress.
