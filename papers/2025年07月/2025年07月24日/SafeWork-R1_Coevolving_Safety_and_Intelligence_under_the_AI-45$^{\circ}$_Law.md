# # SafeWork-R1: 在AI-45$^{\circ}$法则下，实现安全与智能的协同进化

发布时间：2025年07月24日

`LLM理论` `人工智能`

> SafeWork-R1: Coevolving Safety and Intelligence under the AI-45$^{\circ}$ Law

# 摘要

> 我们推出前沿的多模态推理模型SafeWork-R1，它完美诠释了能力和安全性的共同进化。该模型基于我们提出的SafeLadder框架开发，结合了大规模、逐步、以安全为导向的强化学习后训练，并由多原则验证器提供支持。与仅学习人类偏好的传统对齐方法（如RLHF）不同，SafeLadder赋予SafeWork-R1内在的安全推理和自我反思能力，使其能够实现安全领域的“顿悟”时刻。值得关注的是，SafeWork-R1相较于其基础模型Qwen2.5-VL-72B，在安全相关基准上实现了【数学公式】的平均提升，同时保持了强大的通用能力，并在安全性能上达到了与GPT-4.1和Claude Opus 4等顶尖模型相媲美的水平。为进一步增强其可靠性，我们采用了两种创新的推理时干预方法和一种审慎的搜索机制，实现步骤级验证。最终，我们成功开发了SafeWork-R1-InternVL3-78B、SafeWork-R1-DeepSeek-70B和SafeWork-R1-Qwen2.5VL-7B。这些模型充分证明了安全性和能力可以实现协同共进化，彰显了我们在构建稳健、可靠和值得信赖的通用人工智能方面框架的广泛应用前景。

> We introduce SafeWork-R1, a cutting-edge multimodal reasoning model that demonstrates the coevolution of capabilities and safety. It is developed by our proposed SafeLadder framework, which incorporates large-scale, progressive, safety-oriented reinforcement learning post-training, supported by a suite of multi-principled verifiers. Unlike previous alignment methods such as RLHF that simply learn human preferences, SafeLadder enables SafeWork-R1 to develop intrinsic safety reasoning and self-reflection abilities, giving rise to safety `aha' moments. Notably, SafeWork-R1 achieves an average improvement of $46.54\%$ over its base model Qwen2.5-VL-72B on safety-related benchmarks without compromising general capabilities, and delivers state-of-the-art safety performance compared to leading proprietary models such as GPT-4.1 and Claude Opus 4. To further bolster its reliability, we implement two distinct inference-time intervention methods and a deliberative search mechanism, enforcing step-level verification. Finally, we further develop SafeWork-R1-InternVL3-78B, SafeWork-R1-DeepSeek-70B, and SafeWork-R1-Qwen2.5VL-7B. All resulting models demonstrate that safety and capability can co-evolve synergistically, highlighting the generalizability of our framework in building robust, reliable, and trustworthy general-purpose AI.
